# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and professional in discussions.
- Keep feedback technical, specific, and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating disagreements.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
